Creative Plots

Build, build, and, well... build.

Welcome to Creative plots, where imagination comes true! Build anything here. You could build a pvp arena, a statue of yourself, or literally anything. In this gamemode, you have /gamemode and /give permissions, meaning you could give yourself powerful. items like the Debug Stick, or the Barrier (which is just invisible bedrock). Claim your free plot, and start building.


To play creative plots, just type in /plot auto to claim a random plot to start building on. I can give you a couple of examples of functionality such as signs. The signs "[gamemode]", "[free]", and "[buy]" are enabled on here for all players. You could also add players to your plot to build stuff by using commands such as /plot add or /plot trust (trust is not recommended to give to all players). I did not create the plugin used for this game, I am just building freely on here. I might recreate the main creative to look cooler.
