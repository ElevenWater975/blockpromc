KitPVP

Fight to the death!

Welcome to KitPVP. Choose a kit and fight to the death. In KitPVP, there are multiple kits to choose from. Pyro kit, default kit, speedy kit, and more. Choose your tactic and fight to the death in a small superflat arena built by ElevenWater975, C0MG, and Doge_for_life

KitPVP is a great multiplayer minigame and yes, it is multiplayer only. We have built the arena to look good for players in the server. KitPVP is open and ready to play for all. I am not sure what to say about this minigame except that is a very good minigame. Join the fun!



Tips and Tricks

You might find yourself getting into the habit of jumping and sprinting. Don't do this while you have the swiftness effect. Just sprint without jumping.

Cheaters will be instantly jailed/banned from the server.

Look for the traps to trick your opponent.

The grappling hook has been added to KitPVP. Use it to your advantage.

We are setting up timely events for KitPVP. use /warp Event once an event is starting. Don't worry, we will notify you in the server when an event is about to start.
