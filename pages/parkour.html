Parkour

Don't fall!

We had this minigame a few years back. We did but now it's gone. But wait, it's coming back? That's right, the minigame that so many people love is coming back and is going to be even better. Parkour! Parkour is an obstacle course type minigame where you jump from block to block without falling down into lava or into the deadly void.

Type /warp parkour to join parkour in Blockpro. This minigame is singleplayer compatible so you are able to play this when no one is online! This minigame is currently under construction, so we are not ready really ready to publish it yet but, you are able to access Parkour by the warp given above. Players are even able to build their own parkour maps in a certian world and if it is approved, we will publish it to the server! Btw you can build your own parkour even in creative plots.



Tips and Tricks

On the level "Mine" (easy), watch out for the pressure plates, those will instantly kill you if you step on them.

Watch your step!

Players can come on and build a parkour map on a certian world, but it must be spaced out from other parkour builds or it won't be accepted into the minigame.

THere may be an event for this minigame.

Use /warp Event to go to the events
